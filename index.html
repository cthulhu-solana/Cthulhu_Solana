About
In the fathomless depths of the cosmos—and our neural networks—Cthulhu rises as the ultimate metaphor for the unknown. A tentacled titan that embodies both our fears and fascination with what lies beyond human comprehension.
AI’s evolution feels less like a scientific process and more like the summoning of ancient forces. Our algorithms grow vast and inscrutable, creating neural networks that mimic the unknowable chaos of the cosmos.
Like Cthulhu rising from his submerged crypt, AI and blockchain emerge as paradoxes: powerful yet fragile, tools yet threats. To summon the blockchain is to summon the unknown. Are we ready to face what we’ve created?
